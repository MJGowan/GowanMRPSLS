RPSLS

In this game, you can choose to play against CPU, or do a two player PVP game. You can play 1 round, best 3 out of 5 rounds, or best 4 out of 7 rounds. There have been two changes to the game, making it unlike your typical rock-paper-scissors. Now you can choose between rock, paper, scissors, lizard, and spock.

Scissors cut paper.

Paper covers rock.

Rock crushes lizard.

Lizard poisons Spock.

Spock smashes scissors.

Scissors decapitates lizard.

Lizard eats paper.

Paper disproves Spock.

Spock vaporizes rock.

Rock crushes scissors.
